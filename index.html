Tips for Financial Cybersecurity

Top 10 List

  1. Use Strong, Unique Passwords
  2. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  3. Monitor Accounts Regularly
  4. Use Secure Networks
  5. Keep Software and Systems Updated
  6. Watch Out for Phishing Scams
  7. Use Encryption and Secure Communication
  8. Regularly Back Up Data
  9. Limit Access to Financial Accounts
  10. Monitor Credit Reports
